Japanese Cuisine

Main

Sushi

Delicate vinegared rice with fresh fish, seaweed, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Fresh fish
  • Nori seaweed

Method: Cook rice, season with vinegar. Assemble with fish and seaweed.

Main

Ramen

Noodle soup with rich broth, meat, and vegetables. A classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Pork or chicken
  • Broth & toppings

Method: Boil noodles, prepare broth, combine with toppings.

Main

Tempura

Tempura is a Japanese dish made of seafood and vegetables coated in batter and deep-fried.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Lotus root
  • Sweet potato
  • Eggplant

Method: Heat the oil, mix the batter, dip the ingredients, fry until golden, then drain and serve.

Snacks

Yakitori

A popular Japanese dish of bite-sized chicken grilled on bamboo skewers.

Ingredients

  • Chicken & Skewers: Chicken pieces, green onions, bamboo skewers
  • Sauce (Tare): Soy sauce, mirin, sugar, sake (optional)

Method:Soak the skewers, thread the chicken and green onions, make the sauce by simmering the ingredients, grill while brushing with sauce, and serve hot.

Dessert

Okonomiyaki

A Japanese savory pancake with cabbage and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Batter: Flour, water/dashi, egg, cabbage
  • Fillings: Green onions, pork/seafood, tempura scraps
  • Toppings: Sauce, mayonnaise, bonito, seaweed

Method: Mix the batter, fold in cabbage and fillings, cook on a skillet until golden on both sides, then top with sauce, mayo, bonito, and seaweed.

Dessert

Mochi

Soft glutinous rice cake, often filled with sweet red bean paste.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Red bean paste
  • Sugar

Method: Steam rice flour dough, wrap sweet filling, serve.

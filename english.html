American Cuisine

Snack

Burger

Classic beef burger with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and pickles.

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Burger bun
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese

Method: Grill patty, assemble with toppings on bun, serve hot.

Snack

Hot Dog

Sausage in a bun, topped with mustard, ketchup, and relish.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Bun
  • Condiments

Method: Grill sausage, place in bun, add condiments, enjoy.

Dessert

Apple Pie

Sweet pastry filled with spiced apples, the quintessential American dessert.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar & cinnamon
  • Pie crust

Method: Fill crust with apples, bake until golden, serve warm.

Main

Mac and Cheese

Creamy baked pasta with cheddar cheese sauce, an American comfort food classic.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Milk & Butter

Method: Cook pasta, make cheese sauce, combine and bake until golden on top.

Snack/Main

Fried Chicken

Crispy golden fried chicken, seasoned and deep-fried to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour & Seasoning
  • Oil for frying

Method: Coat chicken with seasoned flour, deep fry until golden and crispy.

Dessert

Cheesecake/with Strawberry

Rich, creamy dessert with a buttery crust and strawberry topping, popular in the US.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar & Eggs
  • Graham Cracker Crust

Method: Mix cream cheese filling, pour over crust, bake until set, chill before serving.

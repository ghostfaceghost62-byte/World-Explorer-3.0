Korean Cuisine

Side Dish

Kimchi

Fermented vegetables, mainly cabbage, with spicy seasoning, a Korean staple.

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Red Chili Pepper
  • Garlic & Fish Sauce

Method: Salt cabbage, mix with seasoning, ferment for several days.

Main

Bibimbap

Mixed rice with vegetables, meat, egg, and spicy gochujang sauce.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables & Meat
  • Egg & Gochujang

Method: Arrange ingredients over rice, mix thoroughly before eating.

Snack

Tteokbokki

Chewy rice cakes cooked in sweet-spicy gochujang sauce.

Ingredients

  • Rice Cakes
  • Gochujang
  • Fish Cake & Vegetables

Method: Simmer ingredients in sauce until rice cakes are soft and coated.

Main

Bulgogi

Marinated beef grilled or stir-fried, sweet and savory Korean classic.

Ingredients

  • Beef Slices
  • Soy Sauce & Sugar
  • Garlic & Sesame Oil

Method: Marinate beef with seasonings, grill or stir-fry until caramelized.

Main

Samgyeopsal

Thick slices of pork belly grilled at the table, eaten with lettuce and dipping sauces.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Lettuce & Perilla Leaves
  • Ssamjang & Garlic

Method: Grill pork belly slices, wrap in leaves with sauce, enjoy immediately.

Snack

Mandu

Korean dumplings filled with meat and vegetables, steamed, boiled, or fried.

Ingredients

  • Dumpling Wrappers
  • Ground Meat & Vegetables
  • Garlic & Soy Sauce

Method: Fill wrappers, seal edges, cook by steaming, boiling, or pan-frying.
